So what's this all about?

Point Me, a free platform where users can see rated places around them. From restaurants and clubs to schools and stores. Click the map to see what is nearby or feel free to leave a rating for a place.

The more you rate places, the better our lists will get. Be as honest as possible so other will know about that place.
